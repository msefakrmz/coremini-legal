TimeOff — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 22 June 2026 · App: TimeOff · Provider: Coremini · Contact: info@coremini.com.tr

In short: TimeOff is built privacy-first. It works on your device, with no account and no servers of ours. Your contacts, location, and call information are processed only on your phone to silence work calls when you're off the clock, and are never sent to us. The few exceptions — address search, maps, ads, and purchases — are explained below.

1. Who we are

TimeOff ("the app", "we", "us") is provided by Coremini. This policy explains what data the app processes, why, and your choices. It applies to the TimeOff mobile application.

2. Our core principle: on-device processing

The app's main job — deciding whether to silence an incoming work call based on where you are and what time/shift it is — happens entirely on your device. We do not operate servers that receive your personal data, we do not have user accounts, and we do not sell or share your personal data for advertising or any other purpose beyond the limited third-party services listed in Section 4.

4. Limited third-party services

A small number of features rely on third parties. When you use them, the data described below leaves your device for that specific purpose:

a) Address & place search — OpenStreetMap / Nominatim

When you search for a workplace address, the text you type and your app language are sent to the OpenStreetMap Nominatim service to return matching coordinates. See the OpenStreetMap Foundation Privacy Policy.

b) Map display — OpenStreetMap tiles

The workplace map loads map images from OpenStreetMap tile servers, which receive standard request data (such as the map area you view and your IP address) to deliver the tiles.

c) Advertising — Google AdMob (free tier, Android only)

If ads are enabled in the free version, Google AdMob may process advertising identifiers and usage signals to show ads, as described in Google's policies. Where required by law, you are asked for consent through Google's User Messaging Platform before personalized ads are shown. Buying Premium removes ads. The iOS version contains no ads.

d) Purchases — Google Play Billing

Premium is a one-time purchase processed by Google Play. Google handles the payment; we receive only whether you are entitled to Premium. We never see your payment details. Your entitlement is tied to your Google account, so reinstalling restores it.

5. What we do not do

  • No user accounts and no sign-in.
  • No servers of ours that collect your data.
  • No selling or sharing of your personal data for marketing.
  • No analytics/tracking SDKs beyond the advertising function above (when enabled).

6. Data retention & deletion

Because data lives on your device, you are in control:

  • Clear the silenced-call history any time from the app's "Who called me" screen.
  • Change or remove your selected work contacts and workplaces in the app.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of its locally stored data.

7. Security

Sensitive data stored on the device (silenced-call numbers, your work-contacts list, workplace coordinates) is encrypted at rest using Android's hardware-backed keystore (AES-256). Automatic cloud backup and device-to-device transfer of this data are disabled so it cannot leave your device through a backup.

8. Children

TimeOff is not directed to children and is intended for general audiences (working adults). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Your rights

Under laws such as the EU/UK GDPR and Türkiye's KVKK, you have rights over your personal data (access, correction, deletion, objection). Because we hold no copy of your data on any server, you can exercise deletion directly by clearing data in-app or uninstalling. For any question or request, contact us at info@coremini.com.tr.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
